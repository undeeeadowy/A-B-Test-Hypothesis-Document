# A-B-Test-Hypothesis-Document
You are working for 'ShopSphere', a growing online retail platform specializing in sustainable fashion. The product team has observed a recent stagnation in the 'Add to Cart' rate on product detail pages (PDPs), despite increased traffic. Analytics suggest that users are browsing PDPs but are not taking the next desired conversion step. The current PDPs feature a standard layout with product images, descriptions, price, and a prominent 'Add to Cart' button.

Your team suspects that certain elements on the PDP might be causing user hesitation or a lack of clarity, preventing them from adding items to their cart. They've tasked you with designing a strategic A/B test to address this and propose a specific change. Before any development or design work begins, a clear and well-justified hypothesis document is required to gain stakeholder alignment.
